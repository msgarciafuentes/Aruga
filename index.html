Services

We focus on supporting migrant workers by providing employment assistance, cultural integration, health and well-being programs, social support, and maintaining connections with their families.

Employment Services

We assist employers in finding highly qualified workers who will be able to become assets to the workforce and contribute to the company's success.

Learn More

Cultural Integration

We offer cultural sensitivity training and community engagement programs to help migrant workers integrate into their host communities successfully.

Learn More

Health and Well-being

We prioritize the physical and mental well-being of migrant workers by offering access to healthcare resources, mental health support, and wellness programs.

Learn More

Problem-solving & Social Support

We assist workers on behalf of their employers to resolve issues in the provided accommodations and their everyday lives for the duration of their contracts.

Learn More

Bringing Home Closer

We help the workers feel a little bit closer to home by delivering Filipino products for their cooking and other go-to items straight to their accommodations.

Learn More

Connecting Through Balikbayan

Another part of the Overseas Filipino Workers (OFW) culture that we address is the "Balikbayan Box" through which they send gifts to their families in the Philippines.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reach out for your services?

To reach out to us, simply scroll down and fill out our inquiry form, and our team will guide you through the process step by step. Alternatively, you can send us a Whatsapp message at +36 70 883 8928, and one of our representatives will assist you with the necessary details and answer any questions you may have. We're here to make the process as smooth and straightforward as possible for you.

How do we support the well-being of workers?

We prioritize both the physical and mental well-being of workers by offering access to healthcare resources, mental health support, and wellness programs. Our goal is to ensure every worker feels valued and supported throughout their employment.

What is included in our cultural integration services?

Our cultural integration services include cultural sensitivity training and community engagement programs. These initiatives help workers adapt to their new environment, build meaningful connections, and contribute effectively to their host communities.
